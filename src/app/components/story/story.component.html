And That's My Story...
2 Years ago I completely changed my profession, I have bachelor's and master's degrees in International Relations and MBA in Management. During my studying at Business and Technology University, the contest for participation in Coding School for Women was announced. I decided to try this opportunity, which drastically changed my life. I was pregnant while continuing this course. Then, After 8 months period of being home with my baby, I started to continue my new career. Soon, I found internship at Dataninja project, which was a really good first experience in Front End programming. After couple of months I started searching for a real job and I found it at Georgia Tech Tbilisi as a Markup Developer.
And about the future...
I believe technologies are the future and hope that it will get us to a better life if we use it in a right way. I don't know what I will be doing after 5 years, but I think that it will be related to technologies. At this moment, I am trying to become a professional Front End developer and mastering my skills in JavaScript as well as JS frameworks (React and Angular). I love this job because you can observe how you are growing every day, you can see the results of your work simultaneously and, what is most exciting, you are your own teacher on this path.
